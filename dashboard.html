BlackRock vs American Funds:

The Key Question
Despite economic times that have greatly damaged most asset managers, why has American Funds been gaining share? What's their competitive advantage?
Our Competitive Hypothesis
Although American Funds holds powerful consumer loyalty amongst older, more traditional investors, BlackRock is uniquely positioned to capture the market of millennial investors whose needs have yet to be addressed.
